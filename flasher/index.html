MonoFrame

Flash the MonoFrame firmware onto your Elecrow CrowPanel ESP32-S3 e-paper display straight from this page — no software to install. You only do this once; after that, the MonoFrame iOS app handles all setup over WiFi.

Steps

  1. Use Chrome or Edge on a computer (Safari and phones won't work).
  2. Plug the frame into this computer with a USB-C data cable.
  3. Pick your panel size below, click Connect, and choose the USB serial port that appears.
  4. Click Install MonoFrame and wait for it to finish (about a minute).
  5. Unplug the frame. Its screen will show Setup Mode — now open the MonoFrame app on your iPhone and tap My Frames → Add a Frame.
Which frame do you have?

If the button says your browser is unsupported, switch to Chrome or Edge.

Trouble connecting? Hold the frame's BOOT button while plugging it in to force download mode, then try again. Flashing at most erases and rewrites the frame's firmware — it can't harm your computer.

MonoFrame is open source — github.com/jbaker00/MonoFrame.
