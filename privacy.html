Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

MonoFrame is built to need as little of your data as possible. There are no user accounts — we never ask for your name, email address, or phone number.

Photos you send

When you send a photo to a frame, the image is converted on your device to a small 1-bit black & white file (400×300 pixels). Only that converted file is uploaded to our cloud storage (Google Firebase, operated by Google LLC) so your frame can download it. The original photo never leaves your device.

Each frame stores only its most recent image; sending a new photo replaces the previous one. Images are associated with a random frame ID, not with you.

Frame credentials

Each frame is identified by a randomly generated frame ID and secret token. The token is stored in your device's Keychain and on the frame itself. Our servers store only a cryptographic hash of the token and a "last seen" timestamp, used to show your frame's status. Your home WiFi password is sent directly from your phone to the frame over the frame's own setup hotspot — it is never sent to our servers.

Advertising

MonoFrame is free and supported by ads served by Google AdMob. On first use, iOS will ask whether you allow tracking (App Tracking Transparency). If you allow it, AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to show more relevant ads. If you decline, you still get the full app — ads are simply not personalized. Ad serving by Google is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Local network access

During setup, the app talks to your frame directly over your local network, and can optionally scan your network to discover frames. This traffic stays on your network and is never sent to us.

What we don't do

  • No accounts, and no collection of names, emails, or contacts
  • No access to your photo library beyond the photos you explicitly pick
  • No location tracking
  • No selling of personal data

Data retention & deletion

Removing a frame in the app deletes its credentials from your device. Each frame's stored image is overwritten on every send. If you would like the data for a frame removed from our servers entirely, email us with the frame ID (shown in the app) and we will delete it.

Children

MonoFrame is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
